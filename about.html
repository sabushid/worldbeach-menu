Our Story

Where Papa Worldbeach Welcomes The World

Worldbeach Burundi was born from a simple dream — to create a place where the beauty of Lake Tanganyika meets great food, warm hospitality, and unforgettable experiences. Located on Chaussee d'Uvira in the heart of Bujumbura, Worldbeach has become the city's most beloved lakeside destination.

Founded by Papa Worldbeach, our bar-restaurant sits on the shores of Africa's longest lake, offering guests a unique blend of authentic Burundian cuisine, international flavors, and thrilling water activities — all under one thatched roof.

Whether you're an expat looking for a Friday sunset spot, a family seeking a weekend adventure, a tourist discovering Burundi, or a local celebrating life's moments — Worldbeach is where memories are made.

What We Offer

More Than Just A Restaurant

🍽

Lakeside Dining

Fresh Burundian cuisine and international dishes served with a panoramic view of Lake Tanganyika. From charcoal-grilled tilapia to our signature Worldbeach Sunset Burger.

🚤

Boat Tours & Jet Ski

Experience Lake Tanganyika like never before. 30-minute guided boat tours with a captain and adrenaline-pumping jet ski rides on Africa's longest lake.

🏊

Swimming Pool

Our paid swimming pool is the perfect spot for families and kids. Cool off after a day in the sun, right next to the lake with a cold drink in hand.

🎵

Live Music

Every Friday through Sunday, Worldbeach comes alive with live music and DJ sets — Afrobeat, rumba, acoustic, and more. The best nightlife in Bujumbura.

🎉

Private Events

Weddings, birthdays, corporate events, and celebrations. Our lakeside venue and dedicated team make every event unforgettable under the stars.

📶

Starlink WiFi

Stay connected with high-speed Starlink internet. Perfect for remote work with the best view in Bujumbura, or sharing your Worldbeach experience on social media.

Our Values

What Drives Us Every Day

Burundian Hospitality

Every guest is family. Papa Worldbeach personally ensures that every visit feels special — from the warm welcome at the door to the last drink at sunset.

Fresh & Local

We source the freshest ingredients from Lake Tanganyika and local markets. Our fish is caught daily, our produce is seasonal, and our flavors are authentic.

Community First

With 40 team members, Worldbeach is more than a business — it's a community. We provide jobs, support local suppliers, and bring people together every weekend.
